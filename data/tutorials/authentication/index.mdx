# Authentication

In this section we shall cover how to use the Mirror World SDK to authenticate
your users into your dApp.

## Authentication Strategies

The Mirror World Smart SDK offers different stategies for authenticating your
users into your dApps.

Users can login using the following strategies:

1. [Social Authentication](/tutorials/authentication/social-authentication)
2. [Email Authentication](/tutorials/authentication/email)
3. [Self-custodial Authentication](/tutorials/authentication/self-custodial-authentication)
